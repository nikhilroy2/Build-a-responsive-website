OWNER FAQS

Answers to your frequently asked questions

As an owner, talking through your questions can often put your concerns into perspective. We’re happy to discuss any specific issues you’re having with your tenants or your property so we can design a plan to better manage your investment. These are some of the questions we hear most frequently.

We inspect your property every six months. The purpose is to make sure it’s being cared for by the tenant, to ensure there aren’t any lease violations, and to look for any maintenance or repair items that need attention. We do an inspection before a tenant moves in and record a video tour of the property. We also take photos of all our inspections for documentation purposes.
