DISCOVER the valley

The Shenandoah Valley is located in the western part of Virginia and the eastern panhandle of West Virginia, between the Blue Ridge and Allegheny Mountains. The region is best known for its natural beauty and Civil War history. The area offers a great deal of outdoor activities, to include: national and state parks, camp grounds, rivers, wineries, Orchards, farmer markets, distilleries and so much more!
